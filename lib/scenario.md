DISPUTE: A SaaS provider, CloudCRM Ltd, experienced a 12-hour service outage on 15 March 2026 affecting its enterprise customer, RetailCo plc. RetailCo claims it lost £180,000 in sales during the outage and demands compensation. CloudCRM points to clause 14.3 of the SLA, a force majeure provision covering "third-party infrastructure failures," and notes that the outage was caused by an upstream cloud provider's regional failure. RetailCo argues that clause 14.3 covers natural disasters and similar events, not routine cloud infrastructure issues, and that CloudCRM should have had failover. The parties wish to mediate before considering litigation.

ROLES LIKELY NEEDED: vendor counsel, customer counsel, mediator. Other parties may participate if they have legitimate interest. No role is assigned to anyone.
